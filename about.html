About Our Department

Leading innovation in computing education and research

Welcome to Our Department

Welcome to the Department of Computing and Informatics at Bugema University! As we navigate the rapidly evolving landscape of technology, our Department remains committed to providing exceptional education that prepares students for successful careers in the digital age.

Our comprehensive programs, combined with hands-on learning experiences and industry partnerships, ensure that our graduates are well-equipped to meet the challenges and opportunities of the technology sector. We invite you to explore our programs and join us in shaping the future of computing.

📖 Our Story

Established Excellence

The Department of Computing and Informatics was established in 2010 with a vision to become a center of excellence in computing education and research in East Africa. Over the past decade, we have grown from a small department with 50 students to a thriving academic community with over 500 students and 50+ faculty members.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in computing and informatics that prepares students with modern digital skills for the future, while conducting innovative research that addresses real-world challenges.

Our Vision

To be a leading department of computing and informatics in Africa, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.
