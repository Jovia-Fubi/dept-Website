🎓 Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Computer Science

Duration: 4 Years

Department: Computer Science

Total Credits: 144 credit hours

A comprehensive program covering theoretical foundations and practical applications of computer science, preparing students for careers in software development, AI, and research.

Course Outline:

Year 1 - Foundation Courses
  • Introduction to Programming (CSC 101) - 4 credits
  • Discrete Mathematics (MAT 101) - 3 credits
  • Computer Organization (CSC 102) - 3 credits
  • Digital Logic Design (CSC 103) - 3 credits
  • Calculus I (MAT 102) - 4 credits
  • Communication Skills (COM 101) - 3 credits
Year 2 - Core Computer Science
  • Data Structures & Algorithms (CSC 201) - 4 credits
  • Database Systems (CSC 202) - 3 credits
  • Computer Networks (CSC 203) - 3 credits
  • Operating Systems (CSC 204) - 3 credits
  • Linear Algebra (MAT 201) - 3 credits
  • Probability & Statistics (MAT 202) - 3 credits
Year 3 - Advanced Topics
  • Artificial Intelligence (CSC 301) - 4 credits
  • Software Engineering (CSC 302) - 3 credits
  • Computer Graphics (CSC 303) - 3 credits
  • Theory of Computation (CSC 304) - 3 credits
  • Web Technologies (CSC 305) - 3 credits
  • Research Methodology (CSC 306) - 2 credits
Year 4 - Specialization & Project
  • Machine Learning (CSC 401) - 4 credits
  • Computer Security (CSC 402) - 3 credits
  • Distributed Systems (CSC 403) - 3 credits
  • Final Year Project (CSC 404) - 6 credits
  • Industrial Training (CSC 405) - 3 credits
  • Entrepreneurship (ENT 401) - 2 credits

Admission Requirements:

  • UACE with at least 2 principal passes
  • Principal passes in Mathematics and Physics/Computer Science
  • Minimum of 2 subsidiary passes
  • UCE with at least 5 passes, including Mathematics and English
  • Credit passes in O-Level Mathematics and English

Graduation Requirements:

  • Complete all 144 credit hours
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.75
  • Successful completion of final year project
  • Completion of 8 weeks industrial training
