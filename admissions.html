📋 Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

Direct Entry Scheme

  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 2 principal passes
  • Principal passes in Mathematics and Physics/Computer Science/Economics
  • Minimum of 2 subsidiary passes
  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes
  • Credit passes in Mathematics and English

Diploma Holders Scheme

  • Diploma in Computer Science/IT/Related field from recognized institution
  • Minimum GPA of 2.75 or equivalent
  • Relevant work experience (minimum 1 year)
  • Transcripts and certificate verification required

Postgraduate Programs

Master's Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field from recognized university
  • Minimum GPA of 2.75 or equivalent
  • 2 letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose (500 words)
  • Curriculum vitae (CV)
  • English proficiency test (for international students)

PhD Programs

  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Research proposal (5-10 pages)
  • Academic publications (if any)
  • 3 letters of recommendation
  • Preliminary interview with department
  • Research experience preferred

📝 Application Process

1

Online Application

Fill out the online application form with accurate personal and academic information.

2

Submit Documents

Upload required documents including academic transcripts, certificates, and identification.

3

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee of UGX 50,000 for Ugandan students or $50 for international students.

4

Interview & Selection

Attend the entrance exam and interview. Receive admission decision within 2 weeks.

🎓 Scholarships & Financial Aid

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Full tuition scholarship for students with excellent academic performance.

  • Minimum UACE points: 15
  • Coverage: 100% tuition
  • Duration: Entire program

Need-Based Financial Aid

Partial financial assistance for students from disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Means test required
  • Coverage: 25-50% tuition
  • Renewable annually

Women in Tech Scholarship

Special scholarship to encourage female participation in computing.

  • For female students only
  • Coverage: 75% tuition
  • Mentorship program included
