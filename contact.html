Campus Location

Bugema University Main Campus

Located 30km from Kampala City Center

GPS Coordinates: 0.3476° N, 32.5825° E

🚗 Directions from Kampala:

  1. Head north on Kampala-Jinja Highway (A109)
  2. Turn left at Kira Road junction
  3. Continue for 15km until you see Bugema University signs
  4. Turn right and follow campus signs to main entrance

🚌 Public Transport:

  • Taxi: UGX 4,000-5,000 from Kampala
  • University Shuttle: Available on weekdays (contact for schedule)

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

Visit our admissions page for detailed application instructions and online application form. Applications are accepted throughout the year.

What are the admission requirements?

Requirements vary by program. Generally, UACE with principal passes in Mathematics and Physics/Computer Science for undergraduate programs.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer various scholarships based on academic merit, financial need, and special categories like women in tech.

Can I visit the campus?

Campus tours are available Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM. Please contact us to schedule a visit.
